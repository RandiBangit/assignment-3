Swimming and Pools

Make a splash, get fit and have fun!

The pools are the places to be this summer. Whether you choose instructional programs, leisure swim, aquatic leadership or permitting a pool, we have something safe for everyone. Swimming is a great way to maintain lifelong fitness and wellness as it is often ranked as one of the best forms of exercise for its aerobic and resistance training benefits.

About

All of our pools are maintained in accordance with established Health Guidelines and Standards, and are chlorinated to maintain safe and hygienic environments. All pools have swimming lessons and fitness classes.
